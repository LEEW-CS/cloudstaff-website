The four questions finance leaders ask first

“Who can see my data and systems?”

Access permissions are defined and controlled by you. Cloudstaffers log into your systems using role-based permissions, least-privilege access, and MFA/SSO defined by you. Cloudstaff devices are monitored and encrypted.

“Will this create compliance or audit exposure?”

Cloudstaff operates under a documented security and compliance program aligned with recognized international standards, regulatory requirements, and industry best practices — and supports client audits with documentation as needed.

“What if something fails — power, network, vendor?”

Facilities include redundant connectivity, backup power, and onsite IT support. Business continuity and disaster recovery plans are tested monthly, with replacement equipment on hand and the ability to relocate staff if needed.

“How is misuse of data prevented?”

Multiple layers of protection: background checks, access-controlled workplaces, restricted workstations, removable-storage restrictions, screen monitoring, role-based access, and configurable approval workflows.
