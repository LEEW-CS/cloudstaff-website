Our story

Pioneering global remote staffing since 2005

Cloudstaff was founded in 2005 by Australian Internet pioneer Lloyd Ernst and expanded into the Philippines in 2010. Over two decades, we've refined a model designed to protect margin while preserving control: we manage recruitment, employment, infrastructure, security, compliance, and ongoing support, so business leaders can reduce fully loaded labor costs without increasing risk exposure.

Our experience spans multiple industries, regulatory environments, and growth stages — from mid-market companies scaling support functions to enterprise teams expanding revenue operations. We understand that outsourcing is a financial decision as much as an operational one.
